Connecting Students

Students in high school can connect with students in college to find out what the college experience is actually like. Individuals can ask questions and read answers written by current students, Skype college students, and even plan visit days where a college student will show them around campus and give the prospective student insider information on everything that goes on! There is no better way to find out if a college has the atmosphere one is looking for.
